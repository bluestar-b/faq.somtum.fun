What's somtum osu! bancho?

We are a simple and fun osu! Bancho led by blueskychan_(Fuse), who started the project in March 2024.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can request a new username in a support ticket on the Discord server. You are limited to one change per month, and the new username should not be a duplicate.

Do you accept donations?

Absolutely, yes. However, we don't take money too seriously because we are working on this project for fun.

Why doesn't my profile picture update after I submitted it?

You might try clearing your browser cache or reloading the page.

How do I change my banner, profile background, or profile picture?

You can change your banner and profile background at https://somtum.fun/settings/custom.
If you want to update your avatar, go to https://somtum.fun/settings/avatar.
